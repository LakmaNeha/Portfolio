  7. Let's speak in Pig Latin

    April, 2021

    Pig Latin is a language code or game and is most often used by children who speak English. The usual rules are: For words that begin with consonants, move the consonant to the end of the word and add "ay." For words that begin with vowels, simply add "ay" to the end of the word. EX: pig Latin would be igpay atinlay.
